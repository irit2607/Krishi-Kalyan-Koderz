Our Plan

This portal will help farmers to take advantage of the schemes availed by the Indian government.

Our portal will incorporate the following feature:

* List of all schemes availed by the government.
* Search form - In this One simply has to answer the given questions in the form and the scheme that suits them best will be shown.
* Automatic updating of schemes - Once this portal is linked to the government, then they can add/ remove, or make changes in any of the schemes in the list.
* Contact Form - A contact form is provided where one can feel free to ask their doubts and can also give their kind feedback!
